About Us

We are a catering service committed to providing delicious food for all occasions.

Our Story

Our catering service was founded in 2010 by Chef Jebon tarul luwis, who has over 20 years of experience in the culinary industry. He wanted to create a catering service that would provide top-notch food and service for all types of events, from small gatherings to large weddings and corporate events.

Mission
Our mission is to make every event special and memorable by serving high-quality food and exceptional service. We believe that food brings people together, and we want to be a part of your celebrations.
Team
Today, our team consists of talented chefs, servers, and event planners who are dedicated to making every event a success. We have served thousands of clients over the years, and we continue to grow and expand our services.
